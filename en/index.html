Transforming the world's health one click at a time

Virufy is a nonprofit research organization developing artificial intelligence (AI) technology to screen for COVID-19 from cough patterns, rapidly and at no cost through use of a smartphone.

Our global impact

Virufy aims to change how healthcare is experienced,
empowering people globally with fast and free disease detection.

No matter where you are in the world, all you will need is a mobile device and the Virufy app.

Our vision

We are collecting coughs to refine our AI algorithm, and will implement a three-step detection process that will help contribute to a quick and easy COVID-19 diagnosis for the benefit of countries across the world, especially low-income countries.

Our story

In March 2020, we pioneered an AI-powered mobile app to detect COVID-19.

Now, Virufighters across five continents are committed to making disease detection instant and free of charge through technological innovation.
