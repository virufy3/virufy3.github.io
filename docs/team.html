Team

Virufy is a Stanford COVID-19 Response Innovation Lab project and is also featured in
One Young World.

Our multidisciplinary team has expertise in data privacy, machine learning, medicine, information security, and mobile app development. We are distributed globally across a dozen countries and come from more than 20 universities including UC Berkeley, Oxford, Harvard, and Princeton.
